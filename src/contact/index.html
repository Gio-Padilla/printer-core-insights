What is 3DBenchy?

The 3DBenchy is a small boat model designed specifically to test the capabilities of 3D printers. People print this model because it includes a wide variety of geometric features—such as overhangs, curves, arches, and flat surfaces—that make it an excellent benchmark for assessing a printer’s performance. Printing Benchy helps users evaluate factors like dimensional accuracy, layer alignment, surface finish, stringing, and overhang handling. Because it’s standardized and widely recognized, it’s also easy to compare results with other printers or settings. Essentially, it’s a “test print” that gives immediate visual feedback about how well a 3D printer is performing.
